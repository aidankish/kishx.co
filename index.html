We provide AI receptionist services that answer incoming calls, respond to customer questions, and book appointments automatically. Our system integrates with existing business software to help companies reduce missed calls, increase bookings, and operate more efficiently.

What it does

  • Answers calls 24/7 and handles common questions
  • Books appointments and captures lead info
  • Sends confirmations by text/email (optional)
  • Transfers to a human when needed

Why it matters

  • Stops missed calls from turning into lost revenue
  • Frees up staff to focus on customers in front of them
  • Improves response time and customer experience
  • Consistent, professional call handling

How it works

Most businesses start with missed-call coverage, then upgrade to full call handling once they’re confident.

1) Connect We connect the AI receptionist to your phone line (forwarding or full routing).
2) Integrate We connect your calendar/scheduling system so bookings happen automatically.
3) Launch We test, refine the call flow, and go live with ongoing support.
